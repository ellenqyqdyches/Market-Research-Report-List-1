<p><strong>The Industrial Lead-Acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FLA batteries</li><li>VRLA batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lead-acid battery market consists of two main types: Flooded Lead-Acid (FLA) batteries and Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) batteries. FLA batteries, known for their robust design and ability to withstand deep discharges, require regular maintenance and ventilation. In contrast, VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, offering advantages like compactness and reduced gas emissions. Both types are widely used in applications such as telecommunications, uninterruptible power supplies, and renewable energy storage due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
