<p><ul><li>Cold Oils</li><li>Semi-hot Oils</li><li>Hot Oils</li><li>Bright Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quenching and heat treatment oils are essential in metalworking processes, categorized into different types based on their application temperatures. Cold oils are used for low-temperature processes, providing rapid cooling without distorting the metal. Semi-hot oils operate at moderate temperatures, offering a balance between cooling and distortion control. Hot oils are utilized for higher temperature applications, ensuring effective heat transfer and hardening. Bright oils are specifically formulated to minimize oxidation and maintain a clean surface finish during heat treatments.</p></p>
