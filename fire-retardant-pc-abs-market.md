<p><p>The Fire Retardant PC-ABS market is primarily segmented into two types based on manufacturing processes: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves forcing molten PC-ABS material into a pre-designed mold, producing complex shapes ideal for automotive and electronic parts. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, involves pushing heated material through a die to create continuous profiles, commonly used for sheets and tubes. Both processes benefit from the fire retardant properties of PC-ABS, enhancing safety and performance in various applications.</p></p>
