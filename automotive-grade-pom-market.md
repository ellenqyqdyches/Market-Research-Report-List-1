<p><p>Automotive Grade Polyoxymethylene (POM) is categorized into two main types: Homopolymer POM and Copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM, known for its high rigidity and superior hardness, is ideal for precision components requiring tight tolerances, such as gears and bearings. Conversely, Copolymer POM offers enhanced toughness and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications exposed to harsh environments. Both types play crucial roles in the automotive industry, supporting the development of lightweight, durable, and efficient components in vehicles.</p></p>
