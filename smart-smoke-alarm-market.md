<p><strong>The Smart Smoke Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart smoke alarm market consists of three primary types: photoelectric, dual sensor, and ionization alarms. Photoelectric alarms detect smoldering fires using light sensors and are less prone to false alarms from cooking smoke. Dual sensor alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies, offering versatile protection against various fire types. Ionization alarms utilize radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires but may trigger false alarms more frequently. Each type serves distinct needs in enhancing home safety against fire hazards.</p></p>
